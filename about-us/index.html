About the School

Badridhar Dwivedi Smriti Public School was established in 2016 with the objective of providing structured, inclusive, and value-based education. Currently, the school offers education from Pre-Primary (Nursery) to Class VIII and follows a curriculum aligned with national educational guidelines.

The school operates from a single, self-contained campus and is preparing for vertical academic expansion by seeking CBSE affiliation up to Senior Secondary Level (Class XII).

Vision

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that enable them to excel academically and contribute positively to society.

Mission

  • To provide age-appropriate learning from early childhood to middle school and beyond.
  • To strengthen foundational literacy and numeracy.
  • To foster critical thinking, creativity, and discipline.
  • To encourage sports, arts, and life skills for holistic growth.

Management

The school is managed by a registered educational society/trust and functions in accordance with statutory norms and CBSE requirements.
