Welcome

Badridhar Dwivedi Smriti Public School is a co-educational English medium institution providing quality education from Pre-Primary (Nursery) to Class VIII since 2016. The school is committed to academic excellence, values, discipline, and holistic development in a safe learning environment.

The school has applied for Fresh Affiliation up to Senior Secondary Level (Class XII) with CBSE and is expanding infrastructure and academic readiness in accordance with CBSE norms.

Key Highlights

Established & Operational

Established in 2016 with Nursery to Class VIII currently operational.

Campus & Land

Single campus with 6–7 feet concrete boundary wall and 10,850 sq. meters of land.

Laboratories

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Composite Science, Computer.

Library

98 sq. meters library with reading facility.

Sports Facilities

Basketball, Tennis, Volleyball, Badminton, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho.

Athletics Track

200-meter track facility via adjacent leased land (approx. 100 meters from campus).

Proposed Streams

Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, Humanities with CS/IT.

Principal’s Message

Education is the development of knowledge, character, discipline, and responsibility. At BDSPS, we focus on building strong foundations, nurturing curiosity, and preparing students for higher learning and responsible citizenship.
