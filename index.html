Welcome

Badridhar Dwivedi Smriti Public School is a co-educational English medium institution providing quality education from Pre-Primary (Nursery) to Class VIII. Established in 2015, the school began classes on 03 May 2016. The school is committed to academic excellence, values, discipline, and holistic development in a safe learning environment.

The school has applied for Fresh Affiliation up to Senior Secondary Level (Class XII) with CBSE and is expanding infrastructure and academic readiness in accordance with CBSE norms.

Key Highlights

Established & Operational

Established in 2015; classes started on 03 May 2016 for Nursery to Class VIII.

Campus & Land

Single campus with 6–7 feet concrete boundary wall and 10,850 sq. meters of land.

Laboratories

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Composite Science, Computer.

Library

198.4 sq. meters library with reading facility.

Sports Facilities

Basketball, Tennis, Volleyball, Badminton, Kabaddi, Kho-Kho.

Athletics Track

Outdoor play area and athletics track/oval as per approved site plan.

Proposed Streams

Science (PCM/PCB), Commerce, Humanities with CS/IT.
