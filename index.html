Welcome

Welcome to B.D.S. Public School, Babaganj, Kunda (Pratapgarh) – a premier institution dedicated to holistic education and all-round development. Founded in 2015 by Dr. Vinay Kumar Dwivedi, BDS Public School (BDSPS) strives to inspire students to “dream more, learn more, do more, and become more” in their journey of life. We are an English-medium co-educational school offering classes from Nursery through Middle and Secondary grades, aligned with the latest National Education Policy (NEP 2020) reforms and NCERT curriculum updates. Backed by the Badridhar Dwivedi Group of Institutions (est. 1972), our school provides a nurturing environment where academic excellence, character formation, and 21st-century skills go hand in hand.

Why Choose BDSPS? We take pride in a learner-centric approach that blends modern pedagogy with traditional values. Some key features that set us apart include:

  • Student-Centered Learning: Supportive and inspirational classrooms encourage young minds to question and explore. Our passionate teachers strive to build confident and creative thinkers, delivering education relevant to students’ future. A low student-to-teacher ratio ensures personal attention and fosters a child-friendly learning environment.
  • Holistic Development: Beyond academics, equal emphasis is placed on sports, arts, and life skills. With 20+ events and activities throughout the year, children engage in literary competitions, cultural celebrations, community service, and leadership opportunities. Our House system and club activities cultivate teamwork and school spirit from an early age.
  • Modern Infrastructure: The campus is equipped with digital smart classrooms (projector-enabled), advanced computer and science labs, and a well-stocked library to support interactive learning. We provide a safe, hygienic setting – including clean washrooms and 24×7 CCTV security – giving parents peace of mind.
  • Technology & Innovation: As a progressive CBSE-affiliated school, we embrace educational technology. A user-friendly Learning Management System (LMS) connects students, parents, and teachers for assignments and resources online. We integrate AI learning tools and STEAM education (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Math) in the curriculum to develop critical thinking and creativity.
  • Values & Guidance: BDSPS is committed to instilling values of discipline, respect, and empathy. Our well-trained faculty not only teach academics but also mentor students for life. From foundational years onward, we provide special attention to each child’s needs. In higher classes, we offer guidance for competitive exams and scholarships (Olympiads, NTSE, JEE/NEET foundations, etc.) to prepare students for global competitiveness.

At BDS Public School, your child will discover a second home where “education is a path towards enlightenment”. We invite you to explore our website and learn more about our mission, programs, and the vibrant community that makes BDSPS one of the leading schools in the region.
